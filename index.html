Our Process

Built by Hand. Finished to Last a Lifetime.

Every Artisan Table Co. piece passes through four precise stages before it leaves our workshop floor.

01
01

Sourcing Raw Materials

We travel to certified sustainable forests and specialty lumber mills across North America and Europe. Every slab is hand-selected for grain figure, moisture content, and structural integrity.

FSC Certified Sustainably Sourced
02
02

Precision Cutting & Milling

Using industrial CNC routers and traditional hand tools in tandem, our craftsmen mill each slab to exact tolerances. Joinery is hand-fitted — no staples, no shortcuts. Every cut is measured three times.

CNC Hybrid Hand-Tool Finish
03
03

Epoxy Pouring & Curing

Our UV-resistant crystal-clear epoxy is mixed in controlled batches and poured in multiple layers — up to 3 inches deep per pour for river tables. Custom color matching available. Cure time: 72 hours minimum.

UV Resistant Bubble-Free
04
04

Sanding, Finishing & Final Polish

Starting at 80-grit and working up to 3000-grit wet sanding, each surface is hand-polished to a glass-like clarity. Options: Matte Oil, Satin Lacquer, or High-Gloss Epoxy Topcoat. White-glove delivery included.

Hand-Polished White-Glove Delivery

The Woodworking Academy

Learn the Craft. Master the Art.

From your first resin pour to running your own furniture studio — our curriculum is built by working craftsmen, for serious learners.

Course Categories

Resin Art & River Tables

Master deep-pour epoxy, color theory, bubble control, and the full process of building live-edge river tables from slab to final finish.

Timber Selection & Joinery

Learn to identify wood species, read grain direction, assess moisture content, and execute mortise-and-tenon, dovetail, and spline joints.

CNC Machining & Design

Introduction to CAD/CAM software, CNC router programming, and translating digital designs into physical furniture components.

Workshop Business & Sales

Price your builds, photograph furniture professionally, sell on your own platform, and turn your craft into a full-time income stream.
