Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Nilal Arts, we collect personal information that you provide to us directly. This includes:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, and phone number when you request a custom quote or join the Academy.
  • Shipping Information: Physical address for delivering raw materials or custom bulky table crates.
  • Academy Data: Account credentials, course progress, and project submissions within the student portal.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to improve your experience with us:

  • Processing and fulfilling your table commissions or material orders.
  • Delivering digital access to our resin art courses.
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance for your projects.
  • Sending updates regarding your order status, new workshop launches, or material restocks (you may opt out of marketing communications at any time).

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access. Your payment information is encrypted and securely processed; Nilal Arts does not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. We only share necessary information with trusted third-party partners to complete your transaction. This includes our secure payment gateways and specialized shipping partners responsible for safely transporting delicate epoxy tables globally.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. You may also request corrections to any inaccurate data or ask us to delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at nilalarts@gmail.com.
