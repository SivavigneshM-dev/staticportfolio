2. Custom Table Orders & Variations

All our epoxy resin tables are handcrafted and made to order. Because of the natural characteristics of wood and the fluid nature of epoxy resin, please note the following:

  • Wood Grain & Color: No two slabs of wood are identical. Variations in grain pattern, color, and natural edges are to be expected.
  • Epoxy Behavior: Resin flows and cures uniquely in every pour. Exact replication of past tables or digital renderings is not possible. Minor micro-bubbles or subtle pigment shifts are marks of authentic handcrafting.
  • Lead Times: Production timelines are estimates (typically 8–12 weeks) and may vary based on material sourcing and curing conditions.
