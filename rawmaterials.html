Your Questions, Answered

What is the difference between tabletop and deep pour resin?
Tabletop epoxy is thick, meant for thin 3mm flood coats, and cures fast (24h). Deep pour resin is thin like water, releases bubbles easily, can be poured up to 5cm thick, and takes 72+ hours to cure.
How much resin do I need for a standard 4×2 ft dining table?
It depends on the width and depth of the river channel. You can use our Resin Quantity Calculator above to find the exact kg needed based on your specific slab gap.
Are these materials safe to use indoors at home?
We recommend our Low-VOC Indoor Resin for home use as it has minimal odor. However, standard safety practices — such as wearing the N95 Respirator Mask and working in a ventilated area — are always required.
How long does epoxy take to fully cure?
It varies by product. Tabletop epoxy is touch-dry in 12–24 hours and fully cured in 72 hours. Deep pour resin can take 3 to 5 days to fully cure depending on room temperature and pour depth.
Can I buy these materials without attending a course?
Yes! Our store is open to the public. However, enrolled Academy students do receive an exclusive lifelong discount on all materials.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing for studio owners?
Absolutely. If you run a professional studio, please contact us on WhatsApp for bulk 15kg/30kg commercial pricing rates.
Which pigments work best to achieve the Nilal Arts ocean look?
We combine our Alcohol Inks for fluid gradients and the Ocean Foam Paste to create the signature lacing white crests. The technique is taught in detail in our Ocean Additives starter guide.
Do you ship materials outside India?
Currently, we only ship liquid resins within India due to international chemical shipping regulations. However, we do ship pigments and dry tools globally.
