Our Mission

Art You Can Live On

For too long, homeowners were forced to choose between furniture that lasts and art that inspires. Mass-produced tables are forgettable. Gallery art is untouchable.

Nilal Arts solves this by fusing ethically sourced hardwoods with precision-poured, UV-resistant resin — creating surfaces as durable as stone and as vivid as a painting. Every piece is a 1-of-1 that no machine can replicate.

Beyond our studio, we run the Resin Arts Academy — demystifying chemical artistry so anyone with curiosity can master the craft.

How It Began

Our Story

2019 — The Spark

Founded in a small workshop in Tamil Nadu, Nilal Arts began as a personal obsession with the fluid movement of liquid resin and raw grain of live-edge wood. Early experiments were messy, costly — and thrilling.

2021 — First Commission

Word spread. The first commissioned river table shipped to a client in Chennai, triggering a backlog of requests that validated the brand's market fit for premium bespoke furniture.

2023 — Academy Launch

Demand for knowledge matched demand for product. The Resin Arts Academy opened, teaching hands-on courses to aspiring artists, interior designers, and hobbyists across India.

2025 — Today

500+ tables delivered. 1,200+ students trained. The studio continues to grow — with every pour as intentional and unique as the last.
